About Me

I'm Kamil Mościszko, a passionate Golang developer focused on performance, concurrency, and modern backend architecture. I enjoy building fast, reliable systems and open-source tools that solve real-world problems.

I've created projects such as MemoryArena (a high-performance memory pool), GoEventBus (a Go idiomatic event-sourcing library), and Thunder (a minimalist gRPC-Gateway framework with WebSocket support). I love working at the intersection of developer experience and system efficiency.

My strengths include building scalable APIs, designing low-latency systems, and contributing to open-source infrastructure libraries.
